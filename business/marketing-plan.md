| **Churn (30‑day)** | **<4 %** | Demonstrates product‑market fit. |
| **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** | **≥55** | Strong advocacy for community‑driven growth. |
| **CAC Payback Period** | **≤2 months** | Ensures sustainable unit economics. |

*If any metric falls >15 % short of target, trigger a rapid‑experiment sprint (A/B pricing, additional channel spend, or feature tweak).*
