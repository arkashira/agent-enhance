## Customer Journey – **agent‑enhance**  

| Phase | Trigger Event | Primary Friction Points | User Emotions | Opportunities to Delight | Success Metric |
|-------|---------------|--------------------------|---------------|--------------------------|----------------|
| **Aware** | • Reads a blog post / forum thread about extending Agent‑Reach capabilities.<br>• Receives a LinkedIn ad highlighting “Unlock premium plugins for Agent‑Reach”. | • Low visibility of the paid add‑on (only mentioned in README).<br>• Unclear ROI of a paid extension vs open‑source core. | Curious → Skeptical | • Publish a concise one‑pager with a “30‑second value calculator”.<br>• Offer a free webinar demo with live Q&A. | **Impression‑to‑click rate** ≥ 12 % on awareness assets. |
| **Consider** | • Visits the agent‑enhance landing page after clicking the ad.<br>• Downloads the free “Feature‑Map” PDF comparing core vs premium modules. | • Pricing page is buried; lack of tier comparison.<br>• Concerns about integration effort with existing Agent‑Reach deployments. | Analytical → Anxious | • Interactive pricing matrix with “Save X hrs” estimates per module.<br>• Provide a ready‑to‑run “sandbox” Docker image for a 5‑minute trial install. | **Landing‑page dwell time** ≥ 2 min **&** **PDF download → sandbox spin‑up** conversion ≥ 25 %. |
| **Try** | • Spins up the sandbox, activates a premium plugin (e.g., “Dynamic Prompt Optimizer”). | • Complex auth flow for trial license key.<br>• Limited documentation for first‑time users. | Excited → Frustrated (if hurdles appear) | • One‑click “Activate Trial” button that auto‑generates a time‑limited license key.<br>• In‑app guided tour with tooltips and sample use‑cases. | **Trial activation → first successful run** conversion ≥ 80 %. |
| **Adopt** | • Deploys agent‑enhance in production and purchases a subscription. | • Billing integration (invoice vs card) confusion.<br>• Need for SLA / support guarantees. | Confident → Cautious | • Offer a “starter plan” with 30‑day money‑back guarantee and dedicated onboarding engineer.<br>• Provide a community‑plus‑enterprise support portal with fast‑track tickets for paid users. | **Paid conversion** (trial → paid) ≥ 35 % within 30 days. |
| **Expand** | • Uses multiple premium modules; team grows usage across projects. | • Pricing tier upgrades not obvious.<br>• Lack of cross‑team collaboration features. | Empowered → Expectant | • Introduce “team seats” and volume discounts automatically displayed in the UI.<br>• Release a “Marketplace” where users can share custom plugins, earning credits toward their subscription. | **Revenue expansion** (upsell/ cross‑sell) ≥ 20 % of existing customers per quarter. |

### Narrative Flow (quick view)

1. **Aware** – The developer discovers a need for richer Agent‑Reach features while browsing community resources.  
2. **Consider** – They evaluate the value proposition through clear comparisons and a low‑friction sandbox trial.  
3. **Try** – A seamless one‑click trial lets them experience a premium plugin’s impact on their workflow.  
4. **Adopt** – Confident in the results, they purchase a subscription, backed by risk‑free guarantees and dedicated onboarding.  
5. **Expand** – As the team scales, they adopt additional modules, benefit from collaborative tools, and become advocates in the marketplace.  

---  

**Key KPI to monitor across the funnel:** *Conversion Ratio (Aware → Adopt)* = (Number of paying customers) ÷ (Unique visitors to the landing page). Target ≥ **3 %** within the first 90 days of launch.
